Importance of Salah in Islam

Salah is a direct link between a Muslim and Allah. Observing prayers at their prescribed times enhances discipline, spirituality, and mindfulness. In Bradford, mosques like the one on Victor Street are pivotal for community worship, offering accurate timings and facilities for prayers.

Daily Namaz Timings in Bradford

  • Fajr (Pre-Dawn Prayer): Starts before sunrise and signifies the beginning of a new day.
  • Zuhr (Midday Prayer): Performed after the sun has passed its zenith.
  • Asr (Afternoon Prayer): Begins when the shadow of an object is equal to its length.
  • Maghrib (Evening Prayer): Observed just after sunset.
  • Isha (Night Prayer): Performed when the twilight has disappeared.

Nawafil Prayers and Their Significance

  • Tahajjud - Timing: Late at night, before Fajr. Benefits: Strengthens your relationship with Allah and brings tranquility.
  • Ishraq - Timing: 15-20 minutes after sunrise. Benefits: Brings blessings and increases sustenance.
  • Chasht (Duha) - Timing: Mid-morning until just before midday. Benefits: A means of charity for every joint in the body.
  • Awwabin - Timing: After Maghrib. Benefits: Serves as a form of repentance and gratitude.
